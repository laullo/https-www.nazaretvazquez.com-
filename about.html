Nazaret Vázquez 1990

Nazaret is a multidisciplinary artist, playwright and theater director. She has worked from performance, mixing different contemporary artistic languages to promote human rights and address economic and political conflicts in different countries around the world in order to make visible situations of vulnerability with social groups. She created the organization Theatre for Roots during the first years of her career, where she has developed most of her first experiences.
